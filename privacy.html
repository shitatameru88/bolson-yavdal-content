Privacy Policy — Болсон явдал түүхүүд

Last updated: 18 June 2026

“Болсон явдал түүхүүд” (the “App”) is a story-reading app published by the developer of shitatameru88.github.io. This policy explains what data is handled when you use the App.

1. Information we collect

  • We do not require an account or login. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or other personal information.
  • On-device data only. The App stores your unlocked stories, read history, and language choice locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

2. Advertising & third parties

The App is free and supported by ads. We use the AppLovin MAX mediation platform, which may serve ads from partner networks including AppLovin, Google AdMob, Meta Audience Network, Mintegral, Pangle, Unity Ads and others. To show ads, these partners may collect and process:

  • Device advertising identifiers (Apple IDFA / Google Advertising ID);
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, coarse location/region);
  • IP address and ad-interaction data, used to deliver and measure ads.

This data is collected directly by those ad partners under their own privacy policies. We do not control and are not responsible for their practices:

  • AppLovin: applovin.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Meta Audience Network: facebook.com/about/privacy
  • Mintegral, Pangle, Unity Ads and other networks: see their respective sites.

3. Your choices (iOS & Android)

  • iOS: The App asks for App Tracking Transparency permission. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized.
  • Android: You can reset or delete your Advertising ID, or opt out of ad personalization, in your device settings.

4. Age rating & children

The App contains horror and crime fiction with violent and mature themes. It is intended for users aged 16 and older. It is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

5. Data retention

We keep no personal data on our servers. App content (stories) is served as static files from our public site; no user data is sent to it.
